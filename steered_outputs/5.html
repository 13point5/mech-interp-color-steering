About Us

Smith & Associates is a family law firm dedicated to providing compassionate, professional, and effective legal representation to clients in matters involving divorce, child custody, adoption, domestic partnership, and more.

We understand the emotional and legal complexities of family law cases, and our team is committed to helping our clients navigate these challenges with care and expertise.

Our Services

We specialize in a wide range of family law services including:

  • Divorce and separation
  • Child custody and support
  • Adoption and guardianship
  • Domestic partnership and cohabitation
  • Pre-nuptial and post-nuptial agreements
  • Child support and spousal support
