About Velvet Echoes

Velvet Echoes is a modern indie band that blends soulful melodies with contemporary sounds. With a sound that's both nostalgic and fresh, we're on a mission to create music that resonates with the heart and soul.

Our music is a journey through emotion, crafted with passion and precision. We believe in the power of music to connect, heal, and inspire.
